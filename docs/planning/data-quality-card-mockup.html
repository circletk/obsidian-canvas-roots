Design Notes

  • Issue sections are collapsible — click headers to expand/collapse (first two shown expanded by default)
  • Sorted by impact — Missing place notes shows most-referenced places first
  • Inline actions — "Create", "Edit", "Set parent", "Review" buttons for individual items
  • Batch actions — Links at bottom of each section open existing modals
  • Progressive disclosure — Show top 3-4 items per section, batch action to see/fix all
  • Other tools — Actions not tied to specific issues (standardization, manual create, templates)
  • Summary bar — Quick overview of total issues at a glance
